## Deprecation Warnings (Documented Exceptions)

Canton 3.4 deprecates the legacy `submitMulti` and `submitMultiMustFail` APIs in favor of the new
`submit`, `submitMustFail`, `actAs`, and `readAs` approach. The following test files have
deprecation warnings:

- `Test/daml/OpenCapTable/TestCouponMinter.daml`
- `Test/daml/Shared/TestUtils.daml`
- `Test/daml/OpenCapTable/TestIssuer.daml`
- `Test/daml/OpenCapTable/TestIssuerAuthorizedSharesAdjustment.daml`
- `Test/daml/OpenCapTable/TestOwnershipProof.daml`
- `Test/daml/PaymentStreams/TestUtils.daml`
- `Test/daml/Airdrop/TestAirdrop.daml`
- `Test/daml/PaymentStreams/TestPaymentStats.daml`

These are warnings only and do not affect functionality. The DAML Assistant itself is also
deprecated and will be replaced by Digital Asset Package Manager (DPM) in 3.5.
